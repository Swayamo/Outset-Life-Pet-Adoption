Breeds Of Dogs

Beagle

Traits

Height:   13-16 in (33-41 cm)

Weight:   Males    22-25 lb (10.0-11.3 kg)
                Females    20-23 lb (9.1-10.4 kg)

Colour:    Tricolor or white in combination with black &                   tan/brown or brown/tan or yellow/white

Coat:    Short haired, hard coat of medium length

Life Span:    12-15 years

Dog (domestic dog)

Golden Retriever

Traits

Height:   Males    56-61 cm (22-24 in)
                Females    51-56 cm (20-22 in)

Weight:    25-34 kg (55-75 lb)

Colour:    Any shade of gold or cream

Coat:    Flat or wavy double coat with good feathering,              dense water-resistant undercoat

Life Span:    12-13 years

Dog (domestic dog)

Pug

Traits

Height:   Males    30-36 cm
                Females    25-30 cm

Weight:    14 and 18 pounds (6.3 and 8.1 kg)

Colour:    fawn-colored

Coat:    smooth and glossy coats can be fawn, apricot              fawn, silver fawn, or black

Life Span:    12-13 years

Dog (domestic dog)

Labrador

Traits

Height:   Males    56–57 cm (22–22.5 in)
                Females    54–56 cm (21.5–22 in)

Weight:   Males    29–36 kg (65–80 lb)
                Females    25–32 kg (55–70 lb)

Colour:    Black, chocolate, or yellow (ranges from pale                   yellow to fox red)

Coat:    Short, dense, weather-resistant double coat

Life Span:    13 years

Dog (domestic dog)

Boxer

Traits

Height:   Males    56–64 cm (22–25 in)
                Females    53–61 cm (21–24 in)

Weight:   Males    30–32 kg (66–70 lb)
                Females    25–27 kg (55–60 lb)

Colour:    fawn or brindle, and white

Coat:    short, shiny, smooth, close-lying

Life Span:   average 9–15 years

Dog (domestic dog)

Bulldog

Traits

Height:    31-40 cm

Weight:    40–55 lb (18–25 kg).

Colour:    red, fawn, white.

Coat:    fine, and the hair is straight

Life Span:    7-8 years
